    Trivia
  1. Sully Erna's birth name is Salvatore. He is from italian descent.
  2. Shannon Larkin played drums for Ugly Kid Joe.
  3. Godsmack first major tour was Ozzfest '99.
  4. Erna is a wiccan, and one of his tatoos is of Animal, The Muppet's drummer.
  5. Although there's a song by Alice in Chains called "God Smack", the band's name
    came up when Erna was making fun of former drummer Tommy Stewart.

Although Slipknot and Godsmack are relatively new to Metal Mainstream, members of
both bands were raised listening to Bay Area Thrash Metal.
Similarly to Bay AreaThrash, they were highly influenced by NWOBHM.
